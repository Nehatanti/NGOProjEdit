Achievements of Swami Vivekananda Yuva Sangha

Celebrating 45 Years of Excellence and Impact

  Reached 20,000+ families with improved chulhas through self-employed workers.

  Installed Bio Gas Plants for 2,500+ families and a 20m³ plant with public toilets for a destitute home.

  Ensured primary education for 500+ poor children and pre-primary schooling for 850+ through 5 Creche Centres.

  Installed 18 improved fuel-efficient crematoriums across 10 Gram Panchayats.

  Built 3 medicinal plant gardens in different higher secondary schools.

  Conducted 250+ awareness camps on women's issues, health, environment, savings, and social empowerment with 10,000+ participants.



Founded in 1974, Swami Vivekananda Yuva Sangha began with a small hut and now serves across 7 districts, promoting education, health, environment, and empowerment inspired by Swami Vivekananda's ideals.
