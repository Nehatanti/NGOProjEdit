Welcome to Swami Vivekananda Yuva Sangha


Founded In 1974

About 39 years past, a group of spirited youth of village Apple and Saira of undivided Medinipur took an oath for doing something good for the men in the path shown by the revolutionary monk "Swami Vivekananda" and his master "Sri Sri Ramakrishna" along his Guru Maa"Sarada Devi", keeping in mind the historical words "To serve men is to serve God". On the 31st May 1974 the youth took an unanimous decision for forming a platform of social service called as " Swami Vivekananda Yuva Sangha". It was an another learning of Swami Vivekananda for doing anything, a solid platform is must and anything should be done with a sound unity under proper planning keeping a particular goal in mind.
